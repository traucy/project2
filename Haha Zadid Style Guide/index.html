What does the company do, or offer?

An architecture studio that allows customers to book office tours. Customers can also locate their buildings throughout the world and book tickets through their site.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

This site allows people to see their works in detail online and showcases their work. For people, it might be somewhere they want to visit when they are traveling.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

As travellers, people want to explore the world, and this does not exclude architecture. Visiting beautiful buildings will give people an immersive and memorable spatial experience.

Wangjing SOHO

Wangjing SOHO is a complex of three curvilinear asymmetric skyscrapers in Wangjing in Beijing, China, between central Beijing and Beijing Capital International Airport. According to Zaha Hadid, the project's architect, it is a "welcome and farewell to Beijing".
